Noble Soul is a unified platform to bring together plasma seekers and donors including entities working in the field of facilitating plasma donation such as NGOs, blood banks, hospitals, etc. It is for use in India and covers more than 300 cities of India.

The patient seeking plasma can register on this App and will be able to see the donors available in his/her city. Similarly, the donors can register on this App and will be able to see the patients seeking plasma in his/her city. The latest registered patients and donors are displayed on the top and search can be conducted based on blood group requirements. Noble Soul is designed to facilitate faster matching for plasma transfer,
