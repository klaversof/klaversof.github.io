Scholar Track

Attendance your school can trust.

The moment a teacher marks attendance, every parent gets a WhatsApp message. No app to install. No account to create. Zero hardware cost. Built for how Indian schools actually work.

⏱️ 30s to mark a class 📲 0 apps for parents 💰 ₹0 hardware cost 🚌 2× bus tracking

Discover how Scholar Track is transforming school attendance — one WhatsApp at a time. Free pilot available for one class.

Visit Scholar Track ↗

💬 No app. No hardware. Just WhatsApp.
Every Indian parent already uses WhatsApp. Scholar Track meets them there — no onboarding, no friction, 100% adoption from day one.

💬

WhatsApp — Zero Parent Onboarding

Parents get a personalised message the moment attendance is submitted. Nothing to download, nothing to configure. Works on any phone, on any network, in any language.

🚌

Bus Safety, Twice a Day

Morning boarding and afternoon drop-off tracked separately. Parents know exactly when their child boarded and when they alighted — eliminating the most anxious moments of a school day.

📊

Live Principal Dashboard

See which classes have submitted and which haven't, in real time. Student history, teacher assignments, bus routes — all in one browser tab. No installation needed.

👩‍🏫

Substitute Teacher Support

Teacher absent? Assign a substitute with one tap. They get immediate class access, every substitution is logged automatically, and your school's branding appears on every parent notification.
